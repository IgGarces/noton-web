NOTON Radar Detector Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

NOTON is a traffic radar alert application that respects your privacy. This policy describes how we access, collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Collection and Use of GPS Location Data

üìç Background Location Access

The NOTON application accesses your GPS location CONTINUOUSLY, even when the application is in the background, minimized, or the screen is off.

Location access details:

  • Data type: Precise GPS location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION)
  • Background access: Continuous location when the app is in background, minimized, or closed (ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION)
  • Frequency: Location is obtained continuously while the radar alert service is active
  • Purpose: Detect your proximity to traffic radars and emit real-time sound alerts while driving
  • Storage: GPS location data is processed ONLY locally on your device and is NOT sent to external servers
  • Third-party sharing: We do NOT share your location data with third parties
  • Retention: Location data is processed in real-time to calculate distances to nearby radars and is NOT stored permanently

Why do we need continuous access to your location?

NOTON is a road safety application designed to alert you about radars while driving. To fulfill this function, the application must:

  • Monitor your GPS position constantly while the service is active
  • Calculate in real-time the distance to nearby radars
  • Emit sound alerts when you approach a radar
  • Function even when the screen is off or you are using other applications

User control: You can activate or deactivate the alert service at any time from the application interface. When you deactivate the service, the application immediately stops accessing your location.

2. Other Application Permissions

Bluetooth (BLUETOOTH_CONNECT)

  • Purpose: Detect connection with vehicle Bluetooth devices to automatically activate radar alerts
  • Use: Only used to identify connections/disconnections of Bluetooth devices previously configured by the user
  • Data collected: Name and MAC address of the connected Bluetooth device
  • Storage: Data is stored locally on your device
  • Sharing: NOT shared with third parties

Notification Access (BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE)

  • Purpose: Read notifications from selected applications (WhatsApp, Gmail, Teams) to reproduce them by voice while driving
  • Use: Optional functionality that must be explicitly activated by the user
  • Data accessed: Text of notifications from configured applications
  • Storage: Notifications are NOT stored, only reproduced by voice in real-time
  • Sharing: NOT shared with third parties

Foreground Services (FOREGROUND_SERVICE)

  • Purpose: Keep the radar alert service active continuously
  • Notification: While the service is active, you will see a permanent notification indicating that NOTON is running

Data collected by AdMob:

  • Device information (model, operating system, language)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate IP address for contextual advertising

GDPR Consent:

For users in the European Union, the application implements Google's UMP (User Messaging Platform) consent system that:

  • Requests your consent before showing personalized ads
  • Allows you to accept, reject, or customize your privacy preferences
  • Complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

More information: See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob Policy

4. Analytics and Error Reporting

Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Analytics: Understand how users use the application (screens visited, most used functions)
  • Crashlytics: Detect and fix application errors automatically

Data collected:

  • Application usage events (anonymous)
  • Technical error reports
  • Device information (model, Android version)
  • Anonymous unique device identifier

We do NOT collect: GPS location data through Firebase. Your location data remains on your device.

More information: Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Radar Database

NOTON downloads a traffic radar database from our Firebase Storage servers:

  • The database contains locations of fixed radars, mobile radars, sections, and black spots
  • It is updated periodically with information from contributors
  • It is stored locally on your device for offline operation
  • We do NOT send your location data to our servers

6. Data Stored Locally

The application saves on your device:

  • Favorites: Locations marked as favorites
  • Routes: History of trips made (optional)
  • Custom radars: Radars added manually by the user
  • Settings: Application preferences and settings
  • Bluetooth devices: List of Bluetooth devices configured for auto-activation

Control: You can delete this data at any time from the application settings or by uninstalling NOTON.

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Local processing of location data (not sent to servers)
  • Encrypted storage according to Android standards
  • Secure HTTPS connections with Firebase
  • No storage of passwords or sensitive data

8. Data We Do NOT Collect

NOTON does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Detailed history of GPS locations
  • Personally identifiable data (name, email, phone)
  • Contact information
  • Call or SMS content
  • Photos or multimedia files
  • Data from other applications

9. Sharing Data with Third Parties

NOTON does NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, EXCEPT:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertising (according to your GDPR consent)
  • Firebase (Google): For anonymous analysis and error reporting
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights
