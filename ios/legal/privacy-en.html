Privacy Policy for NOTON Speed Camera Alert (iOS)

Last updated: November 2025

NOTON is a traffic speed camera alert application for iOS that respects your privacy. This policy describes how we access, collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Collection and Use of GPS Location Data

üìç Background Location Access

The NOTON application accesses your GPS location CONTINUOUSLY, even when the application is in the background, minimized, or the screen is off.

Location access details:

  • Type of data: Precise GPS location (Location Services)
  • Background access: Continuous location when the app is in the background, minimized, or closed (Background Location)
  • Frequency: Location is obtained continuously while the speed camera alert service is active
  • Purpose: Detect your proximity to traffic speed cameras and issue real-time audio alerts while driving
  • Storage: GPS location data is processed ONLY locally on your device and is NOT sent to external servers
  • Third-party sharing: We do NOT share your location data with third parties
  • Retention: Location data is processed in real-time to calculate distances to nearby speed cameras and is NOT stored permanently

Why do we need continuous access to your location?

NOTON is a road safety application designed to alert you of speed cameras while driving. To fulfill this function, the application must:

  • Monitor your GPS position constantly while the service is active
  • Calculate in real-time the distance to nearby speed cameras
  • Issue audio alerts when approaching a speed camera
  • Function even when the screen is off or you are using other applications

User control: You can activate or deactivate the alert service at any time from the application interface. When you deactivate the service, the application immediately stops accessing your location.

2. Tracking and Advertising (App Tracking Transparency)

üîí Tracking Permission

NOTON requests your permission before tracking your activity across apps and websites owned by other companies, complying with Apple's privacy policies (App Tracking Transparency - ATT).

What is tracking?

Tracking refers to linking data collected from the app with data from third parties for advertising purposes, or sharing collected data with data brokers.

Tracking data used:

  • IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers): Apple's advertising identifier, used to display personalized ads
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data
  • Performance Data: Ad performance and app usage metrics
  • Device ID: Unique device identifier for analytics

Your control over tracking:

  • If you ACCEPT tracking: You will receive personalized ads based on your interests
  • If you REJECT tracking: NOTON will display NON-personalized (generic) ads that do not require tracking
  • Change preferences: You can change your decision at any time from iOS Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Tracking ‚Üí NOTON

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

NOTON displays advertising through Google AdMob to offer the application for free.

Data collected by AdMob:

  • iOS advertising identifier (IDFA) - only if you accept ATT tracking
  • Device information (model, operating system, language)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate IP address for contextual advertising

GDPR Consent (European Union):

For European Union users, the application implements Google's UMP (User Messaging Platform) consent system that:

  • Requests your ADDITIONAL consent before displaying personalized ads (only if you already accepted ATT)
  • Allows you to accept, reject, or customize your privacy preferences
  • Complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Remove advertising:

NOTON offers an in-app purchase to permanently remove all advertising. If you make this purchase:

  • NO advertising of any kind will be displayed
  • ATT tracking permission will NOT be requested
  • IDFA or advertising data will NOT be collected

More information: See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob Policy

4. Analytics and Error Reporting

Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

We use Google's Firebase to:

  • Analytics: Understand how users use the application (screens visited, most used features)
  • Crashlytics: Detect and fix application errors automatically

Data collected:

  • Application usage events (anonymous)
  • Technical error reports (crash reports)
  • Device information (model, iOS version)
  • Anonymous unique device identifier

We do NOT collect: GPS location data through Firebase. Your location data remains on your device.

More information: Firebase Privacy Policy

5. In-App Purchases

NOTON offers in-app purchases managed by Apple:

  • Remove advertising: One-time purchase to permanently disable all ads
  • Payment processing: Apple processes all payments. NOTON does NOT have access to credit card information or payment methods
  • Data collected: We only validate the purchase receipt with Apple to verify that the purchase is legitimate
  • Restoration: You can restore your purchases on any iOS device associated with your Apple ID

More information: See Apple's Terms of Use

6. Speed Camera Database

NOTON downloads a traffic speed camera database from our Firebase Storage servers:

  • The database contains locations of fixed, mobile, section, and black spot speed cameras
  • It is periodically updated with information from contributors
  • It is stored locally on your device for offline functionality
  • We do NOT send your location data to our servers

7. Locally Stored Data

The application saves on your device:

  • Favorites: Locations marked as favorites
  • Routes: History of trips made (optional)
  • Custom speed cameras: Speed cameras manually added by the user
  • Settings: Application preferences and settings

Control: You can delete this data at any time from the application settings or by uninstalling NOTON.

8. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Local processing of location data (not sent to servers)
  • Encrypted storage according to iOS and Keychain standards
  • Secure HTTPS connections with Firebase
  • No storage of passwords or sensitive data
  • Purchase receipt validation through Apple's secure servers

9. Data We Do NOT Collect

NOTON does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Detailed GPS location history
  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone)
  • Contact information
  • Call or message content
  • Photos or multimedia files
  • Data from other applications
  • Credit card information or payment methods

10. Third-Party Data Sharing

NOTON does NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, EXCEPT:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertising (only if you accept ATT tracking)
  • Firebase (Google): For anonymous analytics and error reporting
  • Apple: To validate in-app purchases
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights
